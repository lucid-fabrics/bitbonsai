Quick Guide

  • Add time: Click and drag on the calendar to create a window
  • Resize: Drag the edges of a window to adjust start/end times
  • Move: Drag a window to change its day or time
  • Remove: Click any window to delete it
  • Always on: Leave empty for 24/7 encoding (no restrictions)
